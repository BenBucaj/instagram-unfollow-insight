    How to Retrieve Your Instagram Data

    Note: Unfollow Insight is an independent tool that allows you to compare your followers and following lists. It works with Instagram data but is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Instagram.

    1. Navigate to your Instagram Settings and Activity section.
    2. Access the Accounts Center.
    3. Under "Account Settings", select "Your Information and Permissions".
    4. Click on "Download Your Information".
    5. Select "Download or Transfer Information".
    6. Click on your Instagram account under "Your Accounts and Profiles".
    7. When prompted with "How much information do you want?", select "Some of your information".
    8. Navigate to the "Connections" section and select "Followers and Following".
    9. Click "Download to Device".
    10. Under "Create Files to Download", adjust the date range to "All Time".
    11. Click "Create Files".
    12. Allow 5-15 minutes for the requested files to become available for download in the same section.
