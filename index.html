    How to Retrieve Your Instagram Data

    1. Navigate to your Instagram Settings and Activity section. Access the Accounts Center.
    2. Under "Account Settings", select "Your Information and Permissions".
    3. Click on "Download Your Information". Select "Download or Transfer Information".
    4. When asked "Where do you want to get your information from?", click on your Instagram account under "Your Accounts and Profiles".
    5. When prompted with "How much information do you want?", select "Some of your information".
    6. When asked "What information do you want for this profile?", navigate to the "Connections" section and select "Followers and Following".
    7. When asked "What do you want to do with your information?", click "Download to Device".
    8. Under "Create Files to Download", adjust the date range to "All Time".
    9. Keep the format as HTML and media quality as Medium. Ensure your email is in the "Notify" section.
    10. Click "Create Files".
    11. Wait 5 - 10 minutes for the files to be available in the original "Download Your Information" section.
    12. Refresh the page if needed until the files show as "Available for Download". Download it.
    13. You should now have a folder downloaded titled "instagram-(your username)". Open this folder.
    14. Inside, open the folder named "connections". Find the folder titled "followers_and_following".
    15. Upload "followers_1.html" and "following.html" to the corresponding sections on this website.

    ⚠ Note: Unfollow Insight is an independent tool that allows you to compare your followers and following lists. It is not affiliated with, endorsed, or sponsored by Instagram or Meta.

    This tool does not interact with Instagram’s API or store any personal data. It simply processes exported files that Instagram users have access to.

    It works exclusively with publicly available data exported via Instagram’s official download feature.
